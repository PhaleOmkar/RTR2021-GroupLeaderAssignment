+ Order of planets (with respect to the sun):

[0]Sun
[1]Mercury -> [2]Venus -> [3]Earth (Moon) -> [4]Mars (Phobos) -> [5]Jupiter (Europa) -> [6]Saturn (Titan) -> [7]Uranus (Ariel) -> [8]Neptune (Triton) -> [9]Pluto (Charon)

+ Planets and moons:
- All planets but two (Mercury and Venus) have moons. 
- Earth and Pluto have 1 each,
- Mars has 2, Neptune has 8,
- Uranus has 15,
- Jupiter 16 (Jupiter's largest moon is larger than the planet Mercury),
- and Saturn 19.
